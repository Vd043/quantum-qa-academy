# Glossary

> Quick definitions you can skim in seconds.

- **QA SDLC** – The end-to-end lifecycle of QA activities across a project.
- **Test Strategy** – High-level approach to testing a product or release.
- **Test Plan** – Concrete scope, schedule, and resources for testing.
- **CI/CD** – Continuous Integration / Continuous Delivery.
- **LLM** – Large Language Model, a type of AI model for text.
