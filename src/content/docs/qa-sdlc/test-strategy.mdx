# Test Strategy & Plans

## What you’ll learn

- The difference between a **test strategy** and a **test plan**.
- How to align testing with business and technical risk.
- Practical templates you can adapt quickly.

## Test strategy vs. test plan

- **Test strategy** – high-level approach for testing a product or program.
- **Test plan** – concrete scope, schedule, and resourcing for a specific release.

## Key elements of a test strategy

- Scope and out-of-scope areas.
- Quality risks and priorities.
- Types of testing (functional, regression, performance, etc.).
- Environments and data approach.
- Entry/exit criteria.

## Practical checklist

- [ ] Risks and priorities are documented.
- [ ] Scope and non-scope are clear.
- [ ] Types of testing are mapped to risks.
- [ ] Exit criteria are agreed with stakeholders.
